top faqs

  • ¿Qué es 1-2-3 Mouldings?

    It is an innovative and practical system of prefabricated mouldings including 8 foot straight sections, corner sections and special adhesive, which serves to decorate and remodel exterior walls in new or existing buildings. This system is ideal to complement outdoor improvement projects such as painting, window replacement, reestucco or to dramatically improve the architectural image, providing beauty and increasing the value of any building.

  • ¿Donde se instalan las molduras 1-2-3 Mouldings?

    The primary use of 1-2-3 mouldings is to decorate the outer perimeters of windows, doors, driveways, walls, columns, etc.

  • ¿De que están hechas las molduras de 1-2-3 Mouldings?

    The moulding body is polystyrene foam, which is coated with a polymer-modified mortar reinforced with fiberglass mesh. The result is a strong and lightweight moulding at the same time, able to withstand the external environment.

  • ¿Cuántas molduras debo llevar para mi proyecto?
